# How to Get Your Site to the Top (SEO Guide)

Your site is now **SEO-friendly** with meta tags, keywords, and structured data. Use the steps below to improve visibility and rankings.

---

## 1. Set Your Real Website URL

Replace `https://shivanyalifecare.com/` with your **actual live URL** in `index.html`:

- **Canonical:** `<link rel="canonical" href="YOUR_URL/" />`
- **Open Graph:** `og:url` and `og:image` (use full URL to your image)
- **JSON-LD:** `"url"` and `"image"` in the script block

If you use **GitHub Pages**, your URL will be like:  
`https://YOUR_USERNAME.github.io/shivanyaHealthcare/`

---

## 2. Claim Google Business Profile (Very Important for Local)

1. Go to [Google Business Profile](https://business.google.com/).
2. Create/claim your listing for **Shivanya Lifecare Collection Center**.
3. Add:
   - **Address:** 551/994, Govind Nagar, Pawanpuri, Tiwaripur, Lucknow, UP 226005
   - **Phone:** 6388744465, 9336595411
   - **Hours:** Daily 7:00 AM – 8:00 PM
   - **Website:** Your live site URL
   - **Category:** Diagnostic lab / Pathology lab / Medical lab
4. Add photos (center, team, reports).
5. Ask patients for **reviews** – reviews strongly influence local ranking.

This helps you show up for searches like *“lab test near me”*, *“blood test Lucknow”*, *“diagnostic center Govind Nagar”*.

---

## 3. Submit to Google Search Console

1. Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console).
2. Add your **property** (your website URL).
3. Verify ownership (HTML file or DNS).
4. Submit your **sitemap** (if you add one, e.g. `https://yoursite.com/sitemap.xml`).

This helps Google discover and index your pages.

---

## 4. Add a Sitemap (Optional but Good)

When your site is live, create a simple `sitemap.xml` listing your main URLs, e.g.:

- `https://yoursite.com/`
- `https://yoursite.com/tests.html`

Submit the sitemap URL in Search Console.

---

## 5. Content and Keywords

- You already target: **lab test Lucknow**, **blood test Lucknow**, **health checkup Lucknow**, **Regency Diagnostics**, **Govind Nagar**, **NABL**.
- Add a short **“About Us”** section (2–3 lines) mentioning Lucknow, Govind Nagar, and Regency – that reinforces local SEO.
- Keep the **Tests & Price** page and keep the list useful; it can rank for “lab test price list Lucknow” type queries.

---

## 6. Speed and Mobile

- Your site is static (HTML/CSS/JS), so it can be **fast**.
- It’s already **mobile-friendly** (responsive).
- After hosting, check speed with [PageSpeed Insights](https://pagespeed.web.dev/) and fix any major issues.

---

## 7. Get Local Backlinks

- List the site on **JustDial**, **Practo**, **IndiaMART**, and other local directories with the same **name, address, phone** (NAP).
- Ask **Regency Healthcare** to link to your collection center page from their website.
- Local health blogs or community sites linking to you also help.

---

## 8. Consistency (NAP)

Use the **same** business name, full address, and phone numbers everywhere:

- Website
- Google Business Profile
- Directories
- Social profiles

That consistency helps Google trust and rank your listing.
